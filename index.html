Bike Lanes vs Car Lanes

Wonkblog of Washington Post fame recently published a great set of maps showing the bike lanes for DC, Boston, Miami and Seattle. I was inspiried to make my own for the top 20 most populated cities in America.

 

Thie first map for each city shows dedicated bike routes in dark green, bike lanes in light green, and recommended routes in dashed green. The second map shows car lanes in red.
